Pawan Sehrawat

Information

Pawan Sehrawat was born in Delhi on 9 July 1996. He is 27 years old. His father's name is Rajbeer Singh Sehrawat.He completed his secondary education at the government school in Bawana. He was employed at RBI.Pawan Kumar Sehrawat is an Indian kabaddi player who plays for Telugu Titans in the Pro Kabaddi League. Pawan became the most expensive player in the history of the Pro kabbadi league.
